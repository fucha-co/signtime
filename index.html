Signtime

Awesome, easy Sign Language lessons

The internet lets us learn any language online in our own time, except for sing language: the most beautiful and expressive language in the world.

Signtime connects sign language teachers and sign language users with people who want to learn sign. Signtime makes it easy for anyone anywhere in the world to learn and practise sign language. Book a 1-on-1 lesson with the tutor of your choice and they'll teach you what you want to learn. Or book a 1-on-1 practice session with a practice partner who is fluent in sign. Signtime opens a world of new language for you to learn and a world of new work for deaf and hard-of-hearing people.
